We are Tarragon

"Tarragon is a private company providing affordable medical Oxygen and other medical supplies to health facilities in Kenya. We are focused on improving access to quality health for all deserving populations in Africa."

What Do We Do?

Supplies: Hospital furniture, Theater/ ICU equipment, Medical oxygen, Surgical gowns , Drapes, masks, caps, Flowmeters, drip stands Glucometer machines,test strips, Meshes, Gastrotomy tubes, stents, Biopsy guns, Lancets, PPE kits Gloves, high flow nasal cannula ets
